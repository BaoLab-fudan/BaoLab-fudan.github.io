About the Bao Lab

The Bao Lab at Fudan University, led by Principal Investigator Feng Bao, develops general learning approaches with applications in biological and medical studies and drug screens. Our mission is to understand complex systems and leverage computational methods to answer biological questions and improve medical research.

We are always interested in motivated students and postdoctoral fellows who are passionate about interdisciplinary research to join our team and contribute to advancing science.
