About the Bao Lab
Fudan University · Shanghai, China

The Bao Lab at Fudan University, led by Principal Investigator Feng Bao, develops general statistical learning approaches with applications in biological and medical studies and drug screening. Our mission is to understand complex systems and leverage computational methods to answer fundamental biological questions and improve medical research.

We sit at the intersection of machine learning, statistics, and biology — designing methods that can extract meaningful signal from high-dimensional, heterogeneous data. A key focus of the lab is multi-modal and multi-omics integration, where we build architectures that learn joint representations across different data types.

What We Do
  • Develop deep learning and statistical frameworks for multi-modal biological data
  • Apply generative models to spatially resolved transcriptomics
  • Analyze high-content phenotypic drug profiles to understand complex biological systems
  • Build open-source tools for the computational biology community
