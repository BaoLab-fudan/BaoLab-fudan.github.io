Bao Lab at Fudan University

We study complex systems and their applications in biology and medicine.

About

Our lab is led by Feng Bao, PI at Fudan University. We develop general learning approaches for biological and medical studies and drug screening. We are looking for highly motivated students and postdocs to work together.

Team

  • Principal Investigator: Feng Bao

Research Interests

We focus on statistical learning methods and biological applications:

  • Statistical learning:
    • Architectures to disentangle information within multi-modal/-omics inputs.
    • Integration analysis across more than two modalities.
    • Contrastive learning for multimodal data.
  • Biological questions:
    • Quantifying interactions across multiple omics types.
    • Improving the throughput of spatially resolved transcriptomics using statistical methods.
    • Integrating high-content phenotypic drug profiles.

Tools

Our lab develops open-source software to support multi-omics and spatial data analysis:

  • adMDM – tool for multi-dimensional multi-omics data integration.
  • SpatialMUSE – tool for spatial multi-omics data analysis.
