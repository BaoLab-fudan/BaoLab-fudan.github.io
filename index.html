Bao Lab at Fudan University

Computational methods for biology, medicine, and complex systems

We develop general statistical learning approaches and apply them to biological and medical questions — from multi-omics integration and spatial transcriptomics to high-content drug screening.

Research Focus

Multi-modal and multi-omics data integration, spatially resolved transcriptomics, contrastive learning, and drug profile analysis.

Open Positions

We welcome applications from motivated PhD students and postdoctoral fellows passionate about interdisciplinary research.

Open-Source Tools

We share our computational tools publicly — including adMDM and SpatialMUSE — to benefit the broader community.

Location

Fudan University, Shanghai, China. Part of a vibrant ecosystem for computational biology and biomedical research.
