Research Interests

Statistical Learning

  • Developing architectures to disentangle multi-modal or multi-omics inputs.
  • Performing integration analysis across more than two modalities.
  • Creating contrastive learning techniques for multimodal data.

Biological Questions

  • Quantifying interactions across multiple omics types.
  • Improving the throughput of spatially resolved transcriptomics using statistical methods.
  • Integrating high-content phenotypic drug profiles to study complex systems.
