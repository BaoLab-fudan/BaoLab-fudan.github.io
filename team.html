Feng Bao

Principal Investigator

Feng Bao leads the Bao Lab at Fudan University. His research focuses on developing general statistical learning frameworks for multi-modal and multi-omics biological data, with applications in spatial transcriptomics, drug discovery, and complex systems analysis.
